The Five Kanna Products: Concentration and Purity Hierarchy

The product range follows a concentration-and-purification ladder. Full Spectrum (~5:1) preserves all four mesembrine alkaloids in natural proportions — the whole-plant reference. Strong (10:1) concentrates alkaloids 10×, preferring mesembrine and mesembrenone. Ultra Strong (20:1) reaches maximum powder density through double extraction. Happy Calming (~5:1) uses source populations HPLC-verified for elevated mesembrenol — unique in the Kanna market. Crystal Ultimate is the crystalline alkaloid isolate at the top of the purity hierarchy, produced by acid-base crystallisation from Ultra Strong.

Mesembrine Alkaloids: Chemistry and Research Relevance

The four mesembrine-type alkaloids share a benzofuranone scaffold derived from the phenylethylamine biosynthetic pathway — structurally distinct from Kratom's tryptamine-derived indoles and Mitragyna hirsuta's oxindoles. This chemical exclusivity (confined to genus Sceletium) and the published in-vitro activity profiles of mesembrine (SERT inhibition) and mesembrenone (PDE4 inhibition) make Kanna an active area of phytopharmacological research. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Kanna Full Spectrum, Strong, and Crystal Ultimate?

They represent three points on a concentration-purity axis. Full Spectrum (~5:1): lowest alkaloid density, highest ratio fidelity — all four alkaloids in natural proportions. Strong (10:1): medium density, mesembrine/mesembrenone enriched. Crystal Ultimate: crystalline acid-base isolate, highest purity, minimal plant-matrix content. For ratio studies: Full Spectrum. For density: Strong or Ultra Strong. For analytical reference: Crystal Ultimate.

What makes Kanna Happy Calming different from Full Spectrum?

Both use the same low-ethanol ~5:1 extraction protocol. The difference is the starting material: Happy Calming uses Sceletium tortuosum source populations HPLC-verified for above-average natural mesembrenol content before processing. Batches not meeting the mesembrenol threshold are redirected to Full Spectrum production. Happy Calming is the only commercially available HPLC-verified mesembrenol-enriched Kanna extract.

Are Kanna alkaloids related to Kratom alkaloids?

No — they are structurally unrelated alkaloid classes. Kratom produces indole alkaloids (tryptamine biosynthesis: mitragynine, 7-HMG). Kanna produces benzofuranone alkaloids (phenylethylamine biosynthesis: mesembrine class). No structural overlap, no expected cross-reactivity in standard immunoassays. HirsutaLab offers both Kratom and Kanna materials as complementary (not overlapping) research categories.

Which Kanna product is suitable for HPLC calibration standards?

Crystal Ultimate — the crystalline mesembrine alkaloid isolate — is the recommended material for HPLC calibration curve preparation. Its acid-base crystallisation production removes plant-matrix components, providing a more defined and stable weight-based standard than extract-grade products. For relative quantification and batch-to-batch monitoring, Ultra Strong (20:1) is also used.
