The Kanna Premium Extract Ultra Strong is produced through a multi-stage concentration process that goes significantly beyond the single-pass extraction used for the Strong Extract. Each successive concentration stage selectively removes additional non-alkaloid plant material while preserving the inter-alkaloid balance of Sceletium tortuosum's mesembrine family. The result is an extract with substantially higher alkaloid density per gram — intended for experienced researchers and collectors who require a high-concentration reference standard.

Why Multi-Stage Concentration Matters

Single-pass extraction from Sceletium tortuosum removes the bulk of non-alkaloid material in one step, producing a concentrate with roughly 5–10× the alkaloid density of raw leaf. Multi-stage concentration applies additional purification cycles, each removing further plant matrix components. The Ultra Strong typically achieves 15–25× the alkaloid density of raw leaf — a level of concentration that provides a markedly different research substrate from the Strong Extract and enables more sensitive comparative analytical work.

Preserving Alkaloid Balance Through Concentration

The technical challenge of multi-stage concentration is preventing disproportionate loss of minor alkaloids — mesembrenone and mesembrenol — that can occur when aggressive extraction methods preferentially concentrate only the most abundant compound (mesembrine). HirsutaLab's process is calibrated to maintain the natural ratio between the four Sceletium alkaloids throughout concentration, producing an Ultra Strong extract that represents a faithful amplification of the whole-plant alkaloid profile rather than an isolated single-compound product.

Intended Use and Handling

Ultra Strong is not an entry-level product. It is designed for ethnobotanical researchers already familiar with Sceletium tortuosum's alkaloid chemistry who need a high-concentration reference for comparative studies across extract formats. Handle with care due to high alkaloid density. Store in sealed amber glass with desiccant, cool and dark. Use only after establishing familiarity with lower-concentration Kanna formats.

FAQ

What makes Ultra Strong analytically superior to Strong Extract for research?

The 15–25× alkaloid density versus raw leaf (compared to ~5–10× for Strong Extract) makes Ultra Strong the better choice when analytical sensitivity requires higher alkaloid mass per sample volume. For spectroscopic analysis, comparative dissolution studies, or standardised dosage preparation in research contexts, the higher concentration reduces weighing error relative to alkaloid mass.

Does Ultra Strong preserve the full Sceletium alkaloid spectrum?

Yes. Unlike isolates that contain only mesembrine, the Ultra Strong maintains the natural mesembrine:mesembrenone:mesembrenol ratio through a controlled concentration process. This distinguishes it from Crystal extract (which focuses on crystallised specific alkaloids) and makes it the preferred format for whole-spectrum comparative research at high alkaloid density.

Who should not use Ultra Strong?

Ultra Strong is not appropriate for collectors new to Sceletium tortuosum extracts. If you have not previously worked with the Strong Extract or Happy Calming, begin with one of those products to establish your reference baseline before moving to ultra-concentrated formats. HirsutaLab recommends a graduated approach to Kanna extract research.

Can I compare Ultra Strong directly with Crystal Kanna?

Yes — and this is one of the most scientifically informative comparisons available within the range. Ultra Strong (full-spectrum multi-stage concentrate) against Crystal (isolated specific alkaloids) allows direct observation of how the presence or absence of minor alkaloids affects the observable chemical character of a Sceletium extract.
