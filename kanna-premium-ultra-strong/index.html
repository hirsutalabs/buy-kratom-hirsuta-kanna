Multi-Stage Concentration Process

Unlike a single-pass extraction, the Ultra Strong undergoes successive concentration stages that progressively remove non-alkaloid plant material while preserving the alkaloid matrix. Each stage is carefully controlled to prevent degradation or disproportionate loss of secondary alkaloids that can occur with aggressive extraction methods. The result is a dramatically denser alkaloid profile per gram than the Strong Extract.

Research Applications

Ultra Strong is valued by researchers building comparative Kanna extract libraries — where having multiple concentration levels of the same source material allows systematic study of how extraction methodology affects the observable alkaloid expression. It is also used as a reference standard when calibrating analytical comparisons between full-spectrum and isolated formats.

FAQ

How does Ultra Strong differ from the Strong Extract?

Strong Extract uses a single controlled extraction pass. Ultra Strong undergoes multiple successive concentration stages, yielding significantly higher alkaloid density per gram. Both preserve natural alkaloid balance, but Ultra Strong is considerably more concentrated — intended for collectors already familiar with Kanna's properties.

Is Ultra Strong suitable for beginners to Kanna?

No. Ultra Strong is a premium-grade, high-concentration extract intended for experienced collectors. If you are new to Sceletium tortuosum, start with the Strong Extract to familiarise yourself with the material before progressing.

Ultra Strong vs Crystal Kanna — what is the difference?

Ultra Strong is a concentrated whole-plant extract retaining the full alkaloid spectrum. Crystal Kanna is an isolated, crystalline extract of specific alkaloids. Ultra Strong is the better choice for whole-plant research; Crystal suits precision alkaloid isolation studies.

How should Ultra Strong be stored?

In an airtight container — ideally amber glass with a desiccant — in a cool, dark place. Avoid temperature fluctuations and moisture. Properly stored, Ultra Strong Kanna extract maintains quality for 18–24 months.
