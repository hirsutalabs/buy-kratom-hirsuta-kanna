About Kratom

Mitragyna speciosa — A Plant with Deep Roots

The Mitragyna speciosa tree has been part of traditional culture in Thailand, Malaysia and Indonesia for hundreds of years. Farm workers chewed the leaves for stamina during long days in the fields. Today, botanical collectors around the world study this remarkable member of the coffee family (Rubiaceae) for its complex alkaloid chemistry.

HirsutaLab sources both green vein (Green Borneo) and red vein (Red Maeng Da) varieties, giving collectors the ability to compare regional phenotypes and processing methods side by side.

About Kanna

Sceletium tortuosum — South Africa's Sacred Herb

Long before modern pharmacology, the indigenous Khoikhoi and San peoples of southern Africa fermented and used Sceletium tortuosum — known locally as Kanna or Channa — for its mood-supporting properties. The plant's primary alkaloids, mesembrine and mesembrenone, have been the subject of growing scientific interest in recent decades.

We offer four distinct Kanna formats: Strong Extract, Ultra Strong, Crystal Ultimate, Full Spectrum and Happy Calming — each representing a different extraction philosophy and alkaloid concentration level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mitragyna Hirsuta and how does it differ from Kratom?

Mitragyna hirsuta (Kra Thum Khok) is a close botanical relative of Mitragyna speciosa (Kratom) within the same genus. While Kratom contains mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine, Hirsuta's primary alkaloids are oxindoles such as mitraphylline. It is legal in most jurisdictions where Kratom faces restrictions, making it a popular alternative for ethnobotanical study.

What does "nano powder" mean for Red Maeng Da?

Nano powder refers to an ultra-fine milling process that reduces particle size far below standard grinding. The result is a smoother, more uniform powder with significantly higher surface area per gram — a key factor in many research and collection applications.

What is the difference between Full Spectrum and Crystal Kanna extracts?

Full Spectrum Kanna preserves the complete alkaloid profile of the plant, including minor alkaloids and natural co-factors. Crystal Kanna is a highly refined product that isolates and concentrates specific alkaloids into a pure crystalline form. Both have their place — Full Spectrum for whole-plant study, Crystal for precision research.
